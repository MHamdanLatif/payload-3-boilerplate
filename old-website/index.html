About Us

Lateef Properties is the real estate arm of M/S Lateef Builders, a trusted name in Karachi’s construction and development sector since 1962. Built on a legacy of integrity, craftsmanship, and customer trust, Lateef Properties carries this heritage into property sales, investments, and asset management.

Specializing in residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments across Karachi and surrounding markets, we offer clients a curated selection of high-quality properties. Backed by decades of building expertise, our team brings verified information, transparent processes, and value-driven guidance to every transaction.

At Lateef Properties, we believe in long-term relationships over one-time deals, helping people and businesses secure spaces where they can grow, invest, and thrive.
